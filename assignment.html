Steps to Prepare Maggi

  1. Step 1: Boil water
  2. Step 2: Add Maggi noodles to the boiling water
  3. Step 3: Add the tastey masala
  4. Step 4: Stir gently to separate the noodles
  5. Step 5: Let it cook for 2minutes,
  6. Step 6: Cook until water is mostly absorbed and noodles are soft
  7. Step 7: Turn off the heat
  8. Step 8: Serve hot and enjoy maggi
